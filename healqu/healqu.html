  • Advanced Wound Care

    We have harnessed the natural healing power of seaweed with his advanced wound care dressing. With high volume absorbtion, the dressing quickly absorbs exudates while forming a gel-like substrate covering over the wound. This substrate helps maintain a moist environment for ideal wound healing and keeps the dressing from adhering to the wound so it is painless to remove. Our silver particle additive creates an anti-bacterial shield that helps reduce the risk of infection.

    HEALQU® Silver Alginate Dressing is comprised of natural fibers derived from seaweed. It is designed to be super-absorbent. The dressing absorbs exudates and forms a gel-like covering over the wound to maintain a moist environment for healing. The gel-like surface also prevents the dressing from adhering to the wound so it is painless to remove.

    HEALQU® Silver Alginate Dressing is composed of calcium alginate and silver particles. The silver content is 50µg/100 cm2. The silver particles are in the form of encapsulated metallic silver, which is very stable and won’t oxidize even when exposed to light.

  • This type of dressing is best used when the risk of infection is high. Wounds appropriately treated with the dressing are moderately to heavily exuding partial to full thickness wounds including (but not limited to):

    • Post-operative surgical wounds
    • Graft and donor sites
    • Trauma wounds
    • Chemical burns
    • Pressure ulcers
    • Diabetic ulcers
    • Leg ulcers

    • Intended use:

      Direct application to full and partial thickness, exuding wounds. This product provides excellent exudate absorption and maintains a moist, anti-microbial healing environment to promote wound healing.

    • Directions for use:

      1. 1. Clean the wound with sterile saline water and dry the surrounding skin gently

      2. 2. Remove dressing from packaging and apply to wound.

      3. 3. Cover and secure the HEALQUTM Silver Alginate Dressing with a secondary dressing.

    • Indications for use:

      Moderately to heavily exuding partial to full thickness wounds, including:

      • Post-operative surgical wounds

      • Graft and donor sites

      • Trauma wounds

      • Pressure ulcers

      • Diabetic ulcers

      • Leg ulcers

    • Contraindications:

      This product is not intended for use in the following circumstances:

      • third-degree burns

      • dry or lightly exuding wounds

      • individuals with a known sensitivity to alginates or silver

      • to control heavy bleeding

      • surgical implantation

    • Notes:

      Dressing change frequency will depend on patient condition and the amount of exudates. Initially it may be necessary to change the dressing every 24 hours. When exudate quantity subsides, dressing change frequency may be reduced.

      Observe the wound before removing dressing. If the wound appears dry, saturate the dressing with sterile saline water prior to removal.

    • Precautions:

      Systemic antimicrobial therapy should be considered when wound infection is evident. HEALQUTM Silver Alginate Dressing may be used, under medical supervision, in conjunction with systemic antibiotics.

      The dressing may adhere if used on dry or very lightly exuding wounds. If the dressing is not easily removed, moisten it with sterile saline solution prior to removal.

      The dressing performance may be impaired by excess use of petroleum-based ointments.

      For external use only.
