    Frequently Asked Questions

    • What Do You Get On The Consultation?

      We will create a personalized work-back plan with you to help you fulfill on your goals with post-secondary education. We will also share how we might be able to support you with your goals on an ongoing basis.

    • Who Is Eligible For A Complimentary Consultation?

      Students who are in grade 9-12, and university year one are eligible for a free consultation. All students that are studying in North America, including permanent residents, are eligible.

    • How Do We Help Students?

      We give students a roadmap and a proven process to graduate debt-free and pursue their dream career. We guide and support students along their journey.

    • Does The Consultation Cost Money?

      The consultation is complimentary and it is designed to give you a clear plan and an idea of the opportunities available to you. If you are a fit for our program and we think that we can help you achieve results, then we will let you know. If that's the case, we will go over the tuition to get started. If we're not a fit for you, we'll be up front about that and let you know.

    • Is GrantMe A Scholarship?

      No. GrantMe is not a scholarship. We are a platform that supports students in maximizing how many scholarships they can win, and we provide tailored scholarship matches for you to apply for.
